BGHC INTENT TO REGISTER 2020/21 SEASON

We are all eager to get back on the ice in the safest way possible.

Rest assured, the BGHC is working hard on our 2020/21 return to hockey plan under the guidance of the OWHA and the City of Burlington.

See the BGHC Return-to-Hockey Plan 2020-21news article.


CLICK HERE TO REGISTER

We are excited to announce that with the movement into Phase 3 for Ontario, we are able to begin looking ahead and planning for 2020/21 hockey season. We understand all our players and families are eager to return to the ice. How this will look for the coming season is still uncertain and we continue to work with the governing body for hockey in Ontario (OHF) and the OWHA on the details surrounding the return to hockey plans.

OFH Return to Hockey:
https://www.ohf.on.ca/media/tukpds4s/ohf-return-to-hockey_0731.pdf

OWHA Return to Hockey:
http://owha.pointstreaksites.com/files/uploaded_documents/3323/OWHA_Return_to_Hockey_Protocols_July_16_20_updated_2.pdf

The first step in preparing for the 2020-2021 season is INTENT TO REGISTER. Each player/family will be required to create a profile on the OWHA’s new registration platform. This new platform will allow for a more streamlined and efficient approach to signing waivers and expediting registration. We will NOT be collecting fees at this time, however, all players must complete a profile to move towards the next phase of back to hockey for the 2020/21 season.

Please register your player's intended level of play at either House League or Rep.

As a reminder you will register your daughter(s) under the newly adopted Hockey Canada division titles:


U7 (Tyke): 2014, 2015, 2016
U9 (Novice): 2012, 2013
U11 (Atom): 2010, 2011
U13 (Peewee): 2008, 2009
U15 (Bantam): 2006, 2007
U18 (Midget): 2003, 2004, 2005
Intermediate/U22: 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002

http://burlingtonGHC.rampregistrations.com

We are excited and committed to getting all our players back on the ice in a safe and fun environment as soon as possible. We will continue to update the community on next steps as they are made available.

We look forward to welcoming everyone back very soon!
