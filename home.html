BGHC 2020-21 Return-To-Hockey

Competitive (REP) Hockey

  • All competitive teams are in the process of being finalized and will be completed the week of September 26th.
  • Registration will open on September 27th and close October 1st at 11:59pm.  Fees will apply with registration.
  • As a reminder, the OWHA has mandated that TRYOUTS ARE NOT PERMITTED for the 2020-21 season.  Assessment skates will only happen should vacancies exist on a team, and will be handled on a case by case basis.  

House League Hockey

  • House League registration and payment will open on September 27th and close on October 1st at 11:59pm.
  • Teams will be created and notified of ice times leading up to October 12th.
  • Fees will be due upon registration
