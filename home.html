BGHC 2021-22 House League REFUNDS - per player

As the 2021-22 season draws to a close we are able to review the impact on fees and provide refund amounts based on the loss of ice that resulted over the course of the season.  As each division was impacted differently, the refunds are as outlined below per age division:

IP1 $45
IP2 $85
U9 $110
U11 $120
U13 $120
U15 $120
U18 $130

*Note that Rep players will not receive any additional refunds as these were reflected in the second tranch fees collected in 2022.

As the process to refund each individual player (hundreds of individual transactions) is a time consuming process, we ask for your patience as we begin to process these refunds via RAMP.
