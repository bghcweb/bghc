2020-2021 House League Season

In response to the declaration of a second Provincial State of Emergency and increased Covid-19 restrictions by the Government of Ontario, we have had to make the difficult decision to close all House League programming for the remainder of the 2020-2021 season. 

https://news.ontario.ca/en/release/59922/ontario-declares-second-provincial-emergency-to-address-covid-19-crisis-and-save-lives

We remain optimistic that spring training sessions with our BGHC Development Team will be permitted for all our athletes in the coming months.  We will continue to keep all our members informed as we navigate through these new restrictions and beyond.
