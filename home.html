2020-2021 Virtual Annual General Meeting - May 2021

The following volunteer BGHC Board of Director positions will renew at the completion of the 2020/21 season. Visit our resource page for a full description of each role.
  
- President (MUST have a minimum of 1 year as a member of the BGHC Board of Directors. Incumbent does not intend to return)
- Vice President Hockey Operations  (incumbent intends to seek re-election)
- Director of Competitive Teams (incumbent intends to seek re-election)
- Director of Communications (incumbent intends to seek re-election)
- Director of House League Operations (Vacant)
- Director of House League Events (Vacant)

If you are interested in applying for one of these volunteer roles,
please complete your application here no later than May 1st, 2021. 
Voting for all positions will take place during the virtual AGM in May 2021, date TBA.
