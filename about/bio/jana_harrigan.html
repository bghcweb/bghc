Jana Harrigan has an extensive hockey career receiving a scholarship from Ohio State University, holding 2nd for the record of all time points at Ohio State (160) and was awarded the “All American”.

Jana went on to coach the Ohio State Women’s Varsity team (06/07) and then continued her hockey career playing in the CWHL from 2007-13.

Jana is the President and Head Instructor of “Harrigan Hockey Inc” where she provides customized hockey training and leagues for females.
