Andy is a long time Sports Executive where he was Partner/Owner in Canada’s largest Privately owned Sport Marketing and Experiential agency. For 25 + years, he was the Senior VP Sports Marketing, working with the world largest sports properties (NHL, NFL, MLB, CFL, Hockey Canada etc.) and tier 1 athlete’s (Sidney Crosby, Bianca Andreescu, Andre De Grasse, DeMar DeRozan, Mark Messier, Vince Carter, Doug Gilmour etc.)

In addition, Andy spent time working in Hockey Operations with the Barrie Colts in the Ontario Hockey League and was a Partner in Arc Sports Group, an NHL Player Agency. Andy continues to actively consults with elite hockey players on NCAA and Junior Hockey Options and Represents Professional Coaches on Contract Negotiations.

Andy has been a coach in the BGHC for 7 years and loves providing a safe, fun and challenging environment for the Cuda girls to grow their love for the game of hockey.
