Aaron played in the OHL with London and Owen Sound, followed by a four-year OUA All-Star career and Queen’s Cup Championship at Western University. This led to an NHL tryout with the Anaheim Ducks and a professional playing career in the USA and Europe before entering into a Masters Degree in Sport Management.

Since 1999, Aaron has worked in the hockey industry with CCM Hockey (2 years spent internationally as the Head of Hockey in Moscow), Newport Sports Management (NHL Certified Player Agent), and presently the President of the OJHL’s Georgetown Raiders. He has advised dozens of players in the NHL, OHL, NCAA, and OJHL with integrity and honesty and can continue to advise female players and parents on the NCAA and CIS.

He’s been an assistant coach for both of his daughter’s teams (now 13 and 9).
