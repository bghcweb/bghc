Founded in 1996, The Burlington Girls Hockey Club celebrates it's 25th year, providing a safe and fun place for young, female athletes to develop both their skills and their true love for hockey.  The BGHC is a not-for-profit organization run entirely on the good-will and hard-work of all our AMAZING volunteers. 

With 750+ female athletes playing Canada's favourite sport, the BGHC offers programs for girls of all ages and skill levels.  Our programs have something for everyone, with IP1/2 to Senior House League divisions, DS teams, a high-level competitive REP program including 4 High Performance Teams and our exceptional Intermediate AA Team (Jr. Barracudas) competing in the PWHL

We pride ourselves on our OUTSTANDING High Performance Development Leaders and Coaches that provide all our athletes the amazing opportunity to learn from the very best!
