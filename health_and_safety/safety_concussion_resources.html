Concussion / Injury Protocols: Process to identify, document and report


  1. Each Team requires 1 designated person (trainer) to identify, document, report a concussion/injury and confirm return to play
  2. Coaches / Trainer(s) to review the below resources under Coaches/Trainers section on this page
  3. Parents / Players (legal guardians) to review the below resources under Parents/Players section on this page
  4. If a concussion / injury is suspected:
    • Designated person to complete Section A of this form: BGHC - Medical Assessment & Medical Clearance Letter
    • Player / Parent (legal guardian) to review this form and understand the return to play process under Section A: Steps 1 through 4
  5. All forms / documents to be submitted to equipsafety@bghc.ca
    • Equipment Safety Director will forward to the OWHA and Hockey Canada for Record Keeping
  6. Ready to Return to play:
    • physician to complete Section B of this form: BGHC Medical Assessment & Medical Clearance Form
    • player CANNOT return to play until this form is completed and acknowledged by the Equipment & Safety Director
